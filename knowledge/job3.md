About the job
Join us in pioneering breakthroughs in healthcare. For everyone. Everywhere. Sustainably.



We’re offering a unique hybrid Field Services Expert (FSE) role combining technical field service with clinical applications support.

Great opportunity for someone from pathology diagnostics; clinical applications or field engineering who’s ready for a change.

You’ll receive extensive hands-on training in Australia and internationally and support a portfolio of pathology laboratory diagnostic systems across the NSW/ACT region.

Your role:

Visiting customer sites across NSW/ACT which include public/private hospitals and laboratories.
Engineering support - Perform installations, preventative maintenance, upgrades and corrective services
Applications support - Deliver application training, workflow optimisation and ongoing customer support
Build strong customer relationships to improve system performance and patient outcomes
Work closely with internal teams and share customer insights to support product improvements
Adhere to all EHS and compliance requirements

Your expertise:

Bachelor’s degree in science or engineering or a related field.
Experience in application support or field engineering in diagnostics. 
Strong understanding of laboratory workflows and diagnostic technologies.
Happy to work autonomously when trained
Hold an unrestricted driver’s licence
Able to travel across state, interstate and overseas.
Be able to provide evidence of your permanent work rights in Australia.
